Announcements

Thielen Student Health Center Update

The Thielen Health Center near State Gymnasium has undergone some changes in order to better accommodate the influx of students on campus. An interim director of the health center has been working since the beginning of July and additional funds have been allocated to help meet demand. On top of this there have been operational changes and we will continue to evaluate the success alongside the Student Affairs Office as the year progresses.

Osborne Drive Update

Osborne Drive has undergone some major changes over the summer. Sidewalks have been expanded in some areas, and the parking alongside the north side of the street has been removed before 5:30 pm in order to increase the safety of this road. Do not fret, this parking is still available after the arms open at 5:30 pm for late studying and those coming to campus. Fall Events

Student Government will be participating in Destination Iowa State, Late night at the MU, and clubfest during the first 2 weeks of school. If you have ever thought about joining, now is the time and we encourage you to reach out and say hello. We will also be helping welcome students during the first two days of school via dispersion of fruit and maps. Come up and chat with us!
