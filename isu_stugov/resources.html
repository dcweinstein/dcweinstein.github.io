RideShare

RideShare offers faculty, staff, and students a safe place to share carpools and longer rides.

ASSET

The Analysis of Social Services and Evaluation Team (ASSET) funds different human and social services in the Ames area.

Student Legal Services

Funded entirely by the Government of the Student Body, Student Legal Services (SLS) is a legal aid office for students currently enrolled at Iowa State University and registered Iowa State University student groups, free of charge

Student Activies Center

The Student Activities Center (SAC) in the Memorial Union offers countless ways to get involved at Iowa State through leadership, service, arts, entertainment and recreation activities. They also manage the recognition process for over 750 student and campus clubs and organizations.

Student Loan Education Office

The Student Loan Education Office was started in the 2014-15 academic year through a partnership of the Government of the Student Body (GSB), Division of Student Affairs and the Office of Student Financial Aid to be a resource for students to help reduce their educational borrowing while learning how to effectively manage their finances while pursuing a college degree. As a GSB funded office, all services provided to students are free of charge.

Iowa State Daily

The Iowa State Daily is an independent student newspaper serving Iowa State University, that is published in print and online. The paper is published five days a week during the fall and winter semesters, and weekly during the summer

New York Times Access

Get full, complimentary access to The New York Times website on any Web-enabled device. Each time you claim a pass, you will have full digital access to The New York Times for 24 hours on your computer and smartphone.
